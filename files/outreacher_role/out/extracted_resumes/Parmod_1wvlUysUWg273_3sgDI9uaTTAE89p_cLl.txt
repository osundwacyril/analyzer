STUDENT RESULT BOT  

GURU JAMBHESHWAR UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY - May 2023-Present 

The Student Result Bot is a project that enables users to retrieve and view their academic result details by entering their 
roll number. The project includes a user-friendly interface where individuals can input their roll number. It then retrieves 
the corresponding result information from a database or online system, which stores subjects, marks or grades, overall 
percentage, and other relevant data. The bot processes the retrieved data, calculates overall percentages and grades, and 
presents the result details in a clear and concise manner. The project aims to streamline the process of accessing result 
information, saving time and effort for students and administrative staff. It offers convenience, accuracy, and 
accessibility, allowing students to access their results anytime and anywhere without relying on manual intervention or 
physical visits to the institution. Overall, the Student Result Bot simplifies the result management process and enhances 
the user experience by automating the retrieval and display of academic performance details.
